Enable or Disable Song Previews

When enabled, hovering your mouse over a song in your library will play a short snippet of the song as a preview.

Supports PSARC, Sloppaks and loose folder songs.

Missing Song Previews

PSARCs always ship with a Rocksmith-baked preview clip; Sloppaks don't always. This tool finds Sloppaks that do not have a preview and fixes them. Two strategies, picked automatically per song:

  • Paired PSARC — if the original .psarc sits anywhere in your DLC folder, the Wwise-baked preview is lifted out of it. Highest quality — exactly what Rocksmith would play.
  • Generated — if no PSARC is around, a 30-second clip is generated from the song's own audio: starts a beat before the first lyric (or first chorus, or loudest 30s window) with a 1-second fade in and out.

Open Song Preview from the Plugins menu for a per-song view with source indicators and individual Fix buttons.
